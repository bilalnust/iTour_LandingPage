How The App Works?

Sign-Up

User can easily Sign-Up to iTour using email and start using app straight away. It help to save data and help you to keep record of your preferences and history.

Explore

Explore the destinations and sights/hotels based on areas. Our search engine and filter mechanism help you with that.

Plan

Create your trip and start adding sights and hotels to it. This will a fully customized experienced based on your preferences and interests.

Book

You are done with the planning. Right? Now you can go ahead and book the added hotels to confirm your slots.

Active

Ready to go to the journey? Just make your preperations and activate the trip. This allow you to track your progress and and helps you organize things.

Feedback

It is very important for us and stakeholders to get the user feedback about their experience and improve on that.
